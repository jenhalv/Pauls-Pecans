Product:
Our pecans have been organically grown, handpicked, machine cracked, shelled, & rinsed (not soaked).  If you choose to rinse your pecans again (removing any remaining pecan dust, fragments, & shell particles), let dry thoroughly in a single layer on paper towels before storing them.
Storage:
Although pecans are often described as a semi-perishable product, proper storage techniques must be used to maintain good nut quality.  Pecans that wonâ€™t be consumed immediately will stay fresh well over a year if stored in an airtight baggie in the freezer.  Pecans can stay fresh for several days at room temperature in open bowls or for weeks if refrigerated.  Poor storage conditions often lead to darkening of the kernel and rancidity of the oils, thus destroying the delicious natural flavor and aroma of the nuts.
Safety Warning:  Your shelled pecans may contain tiny pecan shell fragments.
