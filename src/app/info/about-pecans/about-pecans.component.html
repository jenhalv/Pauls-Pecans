Paul has spent the past 40+ years perfecting the art of grafting pecan trees.  This page will continually evolve as more information is posted & videos are uploaded.  Below is a list of inexpensive items Paul recommends having on hand to graft pecan trees.
Grafting Kit
  • Tool box
  • 1 ¼" nails
  • Pruners
  • Hammer
  • Flagging
  • Cloth ties
  • Graft wood
  • Pocket knife
  • Wood lathes
  • Aluminum foil
  • Electrical tape
  • Masking tape
  • Rubber bands
  • Sandwich baggies
  • Bow saw/chainsaw
  • Cooler & ice pack
  • Flair writing pen
  • #18 x ⅝" wire nails
  • Glasses (if needed)
