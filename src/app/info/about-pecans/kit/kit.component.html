Grafting Kit Paul recommends having these inexpensive items on hand to graft pecan trees.
  • Tool box
  • 1 ¼" nails
  • Pruners
  • Hammer
  • Flagging
  • Cloth ties
  • Graft wood
  • Pocket knife
  • Wood lathes
  • Masking tape
  • Rubber bands
  • Aluminum foil
  • Electrical tape
  • Flair writing pen
  • Cooler & ice pack
  • Sandwich baggies
  • Bow saw/chainsaw
  • #18 x ⅝" wire nails
  • Glasses (if needed)
